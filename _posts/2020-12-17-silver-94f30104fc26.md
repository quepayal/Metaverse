# Silver

Standing in front of the mirror,

And it's shining.

Trace out all the silvers,

And they’re all fading.

Smiling and shying all this time,

And I get so lost in your light.

Amber on the floor,

With different strokes of your love.

Frozen in the fractals,

Still, I’m aspiring.
